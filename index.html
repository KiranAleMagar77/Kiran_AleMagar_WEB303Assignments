Cristiano Ronaldo

About

Cristiano Ronaldo is a renowned Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Manchester United and the Portugal national team. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest footballers of all time.

Cristiano Ronaldo was born on February 5, 1985, in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. He started his career with Sporting CP and later moved to Manchester United, Real Madrid, Juventus, and then returned to Manchester United in 2021.

Ronaldo's career is marked by numerous club and individual achievements, including winning league titles, domestic cups, and international tournaments with his teams.

His achievements include Ballon d'Or awards, Golden Shoes, and several records, making him one of the most decorated footballers in history.
